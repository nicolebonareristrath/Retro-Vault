Casablanca (1942) — A Timeless Masterwork

Reviewed by A. Kamau · June 2026

The tight script writing combined with flawless pacing makes Casablanca hold up incredibly well decades later. The tension between personal desire and moral duty is executed with absolute precision by Bogart and Bergman.

MUSIC REVIEW ★★★★★

Miles Davis: Kind of Blue (1959)

Reviewed by L. Ochieng · May 2026

An absolute masterclass in modal jazz. By pivoting away from complex bebop progressions toward melodic improvisation over scales, Davis created space that feels uniquely atmospheric and profound even today.
