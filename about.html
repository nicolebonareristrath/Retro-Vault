Our Story

· PRESERVING THE CULTURAL LEGACY

More than a collection — a legacy

Founded in Nairobi, Kenya, The Retro Vault began as a small private archive dedicated to the preservation of early 20th-century physical media[cite: 2]. Over the decades, our mission has expanded to digitize, catalog, and celebrate classic entertainment landmarks across film, vinyl records, and vintage arcade cabinets[cite: 2].

We believe that the design constraints of the past generated unparalleled creativity. From the subtle textures of 35mm film grain to the precise orchestration of early 8-bit sound chipsets, our curated archive ensures these definitive artistic milestones live on for modern creators and historians alike.
